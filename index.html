About Me

I am Dr. Anil Aryal, citizen of Nepal residing in Japan, at the University of Yamanashi. I currently work as a postdoc researcher. I completed a Bachelor's in Civil Engineering from nec, Pokhara University. I have worked as a Civil Engineer and Assistant Lecturer in Nepal. I completed my Master's at AIT in Thailand and Ph.D. at University of Yamanashi.
