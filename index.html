About Me

I am Dr. Anil Aryal, citizen of Nepal residing at Japan, a Researcher at University of Yamanashi, Yamanashi Prefecture. I completed Bachelor Degree of Civil Engineering from Nepal Engineering College (nec), Pokhara University (PU). During the Undergraduate study at nec, I had been involved with DelPHE water supply and sanitation Project as a Research Assistant. Then after, I worked for various organization in Nepal as Civil Engineer and Assistant Lecturer. During the working period in Nepal, I have been involoved in designing and inspection of various district roads. Along with this, I had been involved for various projects. As an Assistant Lecturer, I was involved in tutoring various subjects of Civil Engineering for Undergraduate students of Purwanchanl University in Nepal. After that, I joined for Master of Engineering at Department of Water, Engineering and Management in Asian Institute of Technology (AIT), Thailand. After completion of M.Eng., I went to University of Yamanashi (UY) for persuing Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Interdisciplinary Centre for River Basin Environment (ICRE).
